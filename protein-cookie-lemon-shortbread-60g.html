About Us

Welcome to Food to Nourish! We’re an Australian company dedicated to creating delicious, organic, and nutrient-dense wholefood products. Our mission is to inspire healthier food choices, creating products that don’t just taste great but also nourish the body and soul.

Our journey began in 2013 when Nutritionist Danielle Minnebo—a passionate home cook and real food advocate—co-founded Food to Nourish. What started as a simple idea of hand-rolling bliss balls in her home kitchen soon grew into something much bigger.

Danielle’s nutrition background allows her to create products that both taste good but are also made with integrity using quality wholefood ingredients.
